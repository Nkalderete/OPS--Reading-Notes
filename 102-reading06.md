### What makes the Linksys WRT54GL famous?

- The ability to install open source firmware and it comes from factory with linux based firmware.


### Given it’s age, why do people continue to buy the Linksys WRT54GL?

- It has a low cost, supports open source firmware and the long lifetime of the product


### What does the “WRT54GL” stand for in the name?

- WRT- Wireless router
- 54- Indicates speed (Mbps)
- G- 802.11 (WiFi standard)
- L- Linux


### What did Linksys sell before routers?

- They sold a home networking kit with a 5 port switch and two LAN cards


### When did Linksys preview their first consumer router?

- At COMDEX computer industry conference in November 1999


### Who suggested to name it a “router”?

- Bill Gates


### What happened when Cisco replaced the Linux-based firmware with the VxWorks platform?

- They faced backlash from the open source community in 2005 as well as a lawsuit in 2008 by the Free software Foundation (FSF) resulting from Ciscos failure to provide a source code. 


### Why doesn’t the WRT54GL model need to be modified to abide by the new FCC rules?

- The new FCC rules only apply to 5GHz band and the WRT54GL is only 2.4GHz


### Does the WRT54GL model still get updates from Linksys?

- Yes, it still gets occasional updates.
